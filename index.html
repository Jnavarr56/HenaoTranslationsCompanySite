After growing up and finishing high school in Medellin, Colombia, I considered pursuing a career in law in my native country. Though I had traveled internationally throughout my youth, I had little exposure to Anglophone culture and decided that attending university in the U.S. would be a worthwhile opportunity to develop English language skills while receiving a high quality general education. I enrolled in Manhattanville College (1974) and majored in Political Science. During my time at Manhattanville, I became heavily involved in the Latin American Student Union, harnessing my bilingual skills and knowledge of Latin culture in an American setting.

Post graduation, I attended the graduate International Affairs program at Ohio University and graduated with a Master’s degree in 1978. I worked for in a corporate import-export role for 10 years, using my Spanish skills to communicate with distributors in Spain and throughout Latin America. I began translation work, mainly in transcription, as a contractor for the DEA in the early 1990’s and was promoted to Project Manager for a long term contract of DEA Title III Operations by the late 1990’s. I enjoyed this contract style of work enough to work as freelance phone translator for another contractor that served police departments and hospitals.

In 2003 I obtained the New York State Court Interpreter Certification and began working as a Court Interpreter for the NY Court System in Nassau County. Since then, I have become heavily integrated in this network, working in family courts, supreme courts, and with private attorneys and investigators. Additionally, I maintained contract assignments with a number of important government agencies, including the Department of Justice.

I am familiar with various kinds of police evidence software, and subject matter relating to a very wide range of legal contexts, spanning gang-related criminal cases to international security issues. In 2016, I retired from my NY Court Interpreter position and have switched my operations to contractual work for the Nassau Bar Association (Children’s Panel Expert, 18B Panel Expert) and private attorneys. I have developed a strong working relationship with various prominent legal entities and have an excellent reputation within NY Courts.
